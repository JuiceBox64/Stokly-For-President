About

Stokeley Clevon Goulbourne (born April 18, 1996), known professionally as SkiMasktheSlumpGod (formerly stylized as $ki Mask "The Slump God"), is an American rapper and songwriter. Originally well known and notable for his collaborations with fellow American rapper XXXTENTACION and their collective Members Only, he began to gain popularity in 2017 following the release of his songs "Catch Me Outside" and "Babywipe", which are both certified Gold by the RIAA. He also gained popularity in the underground rap movement with songs like "NewWorldOrder", "Laffy Taffy", and "Take a Step Back".
